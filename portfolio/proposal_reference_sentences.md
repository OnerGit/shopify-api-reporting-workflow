# Proposal Reference Sentences

Use these sentences as building blocks in Upwork proposals. Adapt them to the client's actual request.

- I have a related case study showing a Shopify-style API reporting workflow focused on recurring order, customer, and product reports.
- The workflow is designed for practical handoff: validated data, reporting-friendly tables, CSV or Excel outputs, and a short report explaining assumptions and warnings.
- For a real Shopify store, I would adapt the workflow to the Shopify GraphQL Admin API, required access scopes, cursor pagination, and your store-specific field mapping.
- I would first confirm your reporting definitions, especially date range, refunds, discounts, taxes, shipping, product variants, and customer fields.
- The public case study does not expose production connector code or client data, but it shows the type of deliverables I would prepare.
- This is a good fit if you need repeatable reporting without immediately building a full data warehouse or BI platform.
- I can structure the output as CSV files, an Excel workbook, a SQLite-style handoff database, or dashboard-ready summary tables depending on your team workflow.
